We, leading the light!

authors:
Alfaro, Hilary Mae 
Cuadra, Toni Pauline
Luna, Diana Camela

        Diversion Road is a typical highway that connects Barangay Mojon and  McArthur highway. The road caters the joggers because of its length and the field crops beside it that gives a fresh air to breathe in. Another factor that attract the joggers is its attractive rice field that makes the spectacular view of sunrise and sunset visible. 

        Every morning, especially in weekdays, a lot of students pass by since the school is along the road but when the evening comes most people prefer another way to their homes because of its lack of street lights that makes the road prone to accidents. One of these example was when a student walked by the sidewalk, a man strangely asked for her cellphone and tried to steal it from her. this is a perfect example that we should put attention on because since it is just near the school, we need to also protect the students from unwanted incident. The creekside makes it more dangerous because of lack of railings. The people avoid to walk by beside the creekside because to the foul smell around the area that causes by trash and waste that have been thrown to this creeks. 

        This blog promotes the cleaniness and the safetiness of the road and its surroundings. By also doing this act, the government will get aware about this community problem and for them to make time an action to provide the needs for the road construction.
